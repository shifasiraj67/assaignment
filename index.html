bramshill house

From wikipedia ,the free encyclopedia


Bramshill House, in Bramshill,northeast Hampshire,England,
is one of the largest and most important jacobean prodigy house
mansion in England.it was in the early 17th century by
Baron Edward la Zouche of Harringworth but was partly
influence of the italian Renaissance,which became popular in
England durng the late 16th centuary. The house was designated
a grade 1listed buildings in 1992
